Gen AI Reflection

Used in Ethical response page

I used ChatGPT, to assist in refining the tone of my response to a more formal register for my ethical response and have adhered to the following principles:

- Accuracy, bias, and ethical considerations -

While ChatGPT assisted with stylistic refinement, the underlying content and information were generated by me and reviewed for accuracy, potential biases, and ethical considerations prior to using the AI for tone adjustment.

- Information sources -

As the AI was solely used for tone, the information itself was based on my understanding and verified using ACS.

- Personal understanding -

The AI-generated suggestions for a more formal tone were reviewed and integrated in a way that maintains my original meaning and reflects my personal understanding of the subject matter.

- Tool Selection -

ChatGPT was selected as a tool to support learning by enhancing the formality of the language, without generating the core content or analysis, not undermining my own effort.

- Understanding -

AI was not used to generate any substantive content or solutions requiring my understanding of the topic. Its role was limited to stylistic improvement.

- Sensitive or personal data -

All prompts provided to ChatGPT were focused on the stylistic aspects of the text and did not contain any sensitive or personal data.

- AI Use Guidelines -

I have reviewed and understand the AI Use Guidelines provided in the task sheet and have followed the course profile’s AI expectations regarding the use of such tools.

Default image for user driven dynamic content in reflective design

This task involved a two-stage image creation process, initially drawing a base image and subsequently utilizing Samsung Sketch to image generative AI feature to transform and refine it.

- Accuracy, bias, and ethical considerations -

While the output was visual rather than factual, I reviewed the AI-generated image for potential unintended biases in its stylistic choices or representation. Given the abstract nature of the task, overt biases were not immediately apparent, but the potential for subtle stylistic biases inherent in the AI's training data was considered. Ethically, the use of sketch to image based on my own drawing mitigated concerns around generating entirely novel content without a human starting point.

- Information sources -

This point is less directly applicable to image generation. However, I did consider whether the AI's output aligned with general artistic principles and visual aesthetics I've learned from trusted sources on art and design. The evaluation was based on subjective artistic merit and coherence rather than verifiable factual information.

>

- Personal understanding -

The iterative process of drawing the initial image and then observing and interpreting the AI's transformation allowed for a degree of personal influence and refinement. While the AI introduced significant changes, my initial drawing acted as a constraint and a guide, ensuring the final output retained some connection to my original artistic intent and understanding of the subject matter.

- Tool Selection -

Samsung's Sketch to image feature served as a tool for creative exploration and augmentation rather than a shortcut to avoid drawing altogether. The initial effort of creating the base image was essential, and the AI then provided a new lens through which to view and develop that initial idea. This supported learning about the potential of AI in creative workflows without replacing the fundamental act of artistic creation.

- Understanding -

I have reviewed the AI Use Guidelines and ensured that my use of Samsung Sketch aligns with the expectations outlined. The AI was used as a tool for creative exploration and development of my own artistic input.

- Sensitive or personal data -

The initial drawing and the subsequent interaction with the Sketch to image function did not involve the input of any sensitive or personal data. The focus was purely on visual artistic exploration.

- AI Use Guidelines -

This reflection serves as an explicit acknowledgement of the use of generative AI in this task. Given the nature of the output (an image), formal referencing in a traditional academic sense is not directly applicable. However, this reflection clearly documents the AI tool used and the process involved.
