Gen AI Reflection

I used ChatGPT, to assist in refining the tone of my response to a more formal register for my ethical response.

I have adhered to the following principles:

- Review AI-generated content for accuracy, bias, and ethical considerations: While ChatGPT assisted with stylistic refinement, the underlying content and information were generated by me and reviewed for accuracy, potential biases, and ethical considerations prior to using the AI for tone adjustment.

- Verify AI-generated information using trusted sources: As the AI was solely used for tone, the information itself was based on my understanding and verified using ACS.

- Modify and refine AI-generated content to ensure it reflects personal understanding: The AI-generated suggestions for a more formal tone were reviewed and integrated in a way that maintains my original meaning and reflects my personal understanding of the subject matter.

- Select an AI tool that supports learning without undermining effort: ChatGPT was selected as a tool to support learning by enhancing the formality of the language, without generating the core content or analysis, not undermining my own effort.

- Avoid using AI to generate complete solutions where understanding is required: AI was not used to generate any substantive content or solutions requiring my understanding of the topic. Its role was limited to stylistic improvement.

- Ensure prompts do not contain sensitive or personal data: All prompts provided to ChatGPT were focused on the stylistic aspects of the text and did not contain any sensitive or personal data.

- Understand the AI Use Guidelines in the task sheet and follow the course profile’s AI expectations: I have reviewed and understand the AI Use Guidelines provided in the task sheet and have followed the course profile’s AI expectations regarding the use of such tools.

- Confirm whether AI use must be acknowledged, referenced, or both: I confirm that the course requirements necessitate the acknowledgement of AI use.

- Ensure AI use aligns with disciplinary norms and university guidelines: The use of AI solely for refining the tone of my already generated response and aligns with disciplinary norms and university guidelines for the responsible use of such tools.

The use of ChatGPT was limited to enhancing the formal tone of my writing. The core content, analysis, and conclusions are entirely my own.
