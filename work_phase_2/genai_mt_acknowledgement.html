[Context]

This assessment required getting CHATGPT to act as a character and conduct an interview.


[Checklist]

- Rules and Boundaries:

Based on the assessment requirements, the use of AI is needed

- Tool Selection:

ChatGPT was the most avilable and well used generative AI which gives it some additional credibility

- Privacy Considerations:

All input information was made up

- Reviewing and Verifying Content:

All answers was read through and proof read to ensure that answers made sense

- Ethical Considerations:

I checked through all the information and made sure that no information was offensive or any bisaed assumptions were made

- Acknowledgment and attribution:

A short introduction was added to the section where gen AI was used


[Reflection]

CHATGPT has really helped in getting in the mind of various individuals that i would normally not have the time or resources to be able to talk to

It has also helped in getting a better understanding of the various roles and how they would react to certain situations

One improvement i would like to implement would be to limits the responses as i had some really long content which i think could be shortened

I have used generative AI in this assessment to help me get a better understanding of the various roles and how they would react to certain situations as well as taking steps to ensure that informations was diverse and unbiased.
