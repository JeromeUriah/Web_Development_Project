This is the Ethics Analysis

- The Context -

As part of each website development, we want you to integrate a third-party newsletter subscription plug-in provided by one of our funders. This plug-in is designed to allow users to subscribe to newsletters from the funder. When subscribing, users will be asked to complete the following information:

• Full name
• Email address
• Occupation
• Annual income
• Household size
• Sexuality
• Gender
• Spending habits
• Type of phone they currently use
• Monthly spend on dining out/takeaway food
• Number of credit cards they have with the credit limit on each card

The funder has indicated that this information will help them better understand the website audience for their reporting and tailoring future resources. Please note that you do not need to create this form as the plug-in will automatically do this. Theplug-in allows you to style the form to suit your design for the website.
